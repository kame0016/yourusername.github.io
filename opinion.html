My thoughts on the ICT programme


My thoughts and opinion on the HBO ICT programme are mostly positive
I have only been on the programme now for 2 weeks so i dont know everything
but until now i very much enjoy the programme and i feel very welcome
I like that the teachers sort of guide you a little but make sure that you always try to find everything yourself
this way i become more self sufficient in my opinion


The first 2 weeks of the programme are dedicaded to building this website your looking at right now
At first i found coding very difficult, i had zero experience before with coding or anything related to building a website
but throughout the week i became more comfortable with coding and building the website
in my opinion you need to take the time to learn everthing bit by bit and be very patient because it will mostly never work in one try
I also really enjoy working on the website and writing the code
This is the first time i really enjoyed school and found it really interesting and usefull
