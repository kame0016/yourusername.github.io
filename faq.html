FAQ
here are the frequently asked questions from students and myself with the answers
How can you print a document from your laptop at HZ?


Wil je printen van je eigen mobiel of laptop door de printer te installeren? Ga naar https://hz.mynetpay.nl/ en klik op Mobility Print, kies je besturingssysteem en volg de instructies. Wil je direct printen van je eigen mobile of laptop zonder de printer te installeren? Ga naar https://hz.mynetpay.nl/ en klik op Webprint, selecteer je bestand en klik op uploaden.

How can I buy something (like when I sign up for the IT introduction event) on the HZ web shop?


To buy something on the HZ webshop you have to go to the HZ webshop and select either: HZ, HZ sport or HZ cult here you can place your order, this will then go to your shopping cart and you can finalise the payment

How can you book a project space?


The booking for project spaces is done in the self service portal Go to the home page of the Self Service Portal Click on Reserve Choose the correct category and look for the "resource" you want to reserve. If necessary, use the Type filter to enable/disable certain items in the overview. Select the day you want to book By dragging with the left mouse button, indicate the time period when the resource will be borrowed
